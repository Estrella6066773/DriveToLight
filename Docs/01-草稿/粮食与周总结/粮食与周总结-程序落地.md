### 5.3 建议实现顺序

1. **草稿文档定稿**（本目录五篇）与 O-N1～N8 核对
2. **数据字典**：`FoodAllocationResult`、`AllocationSortKey`、`FoodWarehouseDrawdown`、`EscortAccompanyingState`、`IWeeklySummaryStep`
3. **Allocator 骨架**：`FoodWarehouseDrawdown` → `RunFoodOverrides` → `DefaultProportionalDistrictRule` → `ApplyDeaths`
4. **周总结骨架**：`WeeklySummaryOrchestrator` + `StepCatalog` + `PopulationFoodWeeklySummaryStep`
5. **`ApplyWorkHeadcountChange`**（O-04 定稿后接入）
6. **Turn**：`EnvironmentAction` 后 `orchestrator.Run(ctx)`
7. **UI**：`FoodSufficiencyIndicator` 常驻充足性
8. **文档同步**（§5.1）与 OPEN 条目更新
